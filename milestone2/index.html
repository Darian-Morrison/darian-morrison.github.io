Augmented Reality Campus Navigation

Project Phase 2

Project Phase 2 | Background

This report contains our design implication for a Campus Navigator application. The application will run on Android and IOS Devices. The design choices made were chosen based off of the requirements report completed earlier in the semester (Click here for reference).

Our Campus Navigator app will make campus navigation easier for new and old campus goers. The difference between other navigation is our integration of descriptive floor plans and augmented reality software. Our goal is to make campus navigation more fun and less of chores.
