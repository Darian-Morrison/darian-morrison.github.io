Augmented Reality Campus Navigation

Project Phase 2

Project Phase 2 | Background

This follow is a design implication for a Campus Navigator application. The application runs on Android and IOS Devices. The design choices are based off of users, goals and requirements from the requirements report completed earlier in the semester (Click here for reference).

Our Campus Navigator app makes campus navigation easier for new and old campus goers. The difference between other navigation is our integration of descriptive floor plans and augmented reality software. Our goal is to make campus navigation more fun and less of a chore.
