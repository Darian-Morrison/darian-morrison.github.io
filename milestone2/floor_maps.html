Floor Maps

Design

The Floor Plan menu allows the user to view the various floors of a building. With the additional information of the buildingâ€™s floor plans the user will not only be able to find the building they need to be at, they will also be able to find their way around the building to find their desired room(s).. Floor plans are beneficial for visitors, new and old students, and lecturers. When the floor plans option is selected, the floor plans will overlap the map function of the application, allowing the user to track their position within the building they are in. The floor plan shows: class numbers, stairs, water fountains, washrooms, exits, and accessible routes. On the side of the screen is the ability for the user to change the floor number of the building. The floor plans can be viewed through the building information, the map function,, and the AR function..
