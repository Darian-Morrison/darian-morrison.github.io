Cognitive Walkthrough

When the app is first opened I’m greeted with the main menu. Since I’m looking for ECS 660 I’ll try searching it up. I click the search button which brings me to the search bar. I type in ECS 660 and no results are found. I then try just ECS and get the same result. After I start to write out Engineering a search option for Engineering and Computer Science shows up and I choose it. It navigate to the ECS’ description. I click on Floor Plan which then shows the first floor. Scrolling through the floors I get to the sixth floor and find ECS 660 on it.
