Augmented Reality Campus Navigation

Project Phase 4

Task Description

We asked the users to “find ECS 660” on the floor plan, and to time how long it took them to perform the task to evaluate the efficiency of the application.

The users will click through the three functions of the application (map, search, building identification), and their sub-functions (map: directions, AR mode; search: floorplan, directions; building ID: floorplan, directions) to identify the usability of the application.

While the users are clicking, we recorded how many clicks they took to perform each action.
