Evaluation Strategy

In order to evaluate whether or not the Campus Clairvoyance application meet all the requirements that get mentioned in Milestone 1, we asked the evaluators to perform simple task that requires the evaluators to play around with the application. For the evaluation, we will be collecting both quantitative and qualitative data. In the quantitative data, the questions will check if the application meet the user requirements or not, while, for the qualitative data, the questions will be focusing on what users experience after using the application. The Campus Clairvoyance application that is used to evaluate in the lab is a High Fidelity prototype version.
