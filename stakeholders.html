Augmented Reality Campus Navigation

Your own clairvoyance spell

Stakeholders
  • Users: Including experienced and new students, those employed by the university and others that might be visiting the campus. The product is expected to be an effective tool to reduce time spent navigating and make to make the experience more enjoyable.
  • Developers: Developers are looking to build an effective tool that the users will enjoy. The project will be a good opportunity for the developers to learn about AR and improve their software development skills.
  • Universities: Ideally universities will fund this project as it can be used to help its student body, especially newcomers to campuses around the world.
