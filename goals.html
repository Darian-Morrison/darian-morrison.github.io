Goals

Usability Goals

  • Easy to learn: Is the system intuitive enough to learn without a tutorial?

    We aim to have the application be intuitive. Ideally, the application is easy enough to learn without the use of a tutorial; however, to ensure the applications helpfulness a help section would be included.


  • Easy to remember how to use: Can the system be easily picked up after a long hiatus of use?

    Additionally, the application would be intuitive enough in its use that even after long periods of disuse, the user can easily remember how to use it.


  • Effective at leading user to desired rooms: Does the application successfully lead the client to their destination? Does it do so efficiently?

    We want to focus the direction giving portion of the application to being efficient. We want to ensure that the application successfully leads the user to their destination, using the most efficient routing through campus.

User Experience Goals

  • Makes campus navigation fun: Does the product make campus navigation more entertaining?

    One of our goals is to alleviate the stress of navigating campus by making the app entertaining to the user.


  • Involve creative elements in the application: Is the campus navigator app designed in a creative fashion?

    Using elements like AR and intricate floor are meant to be used in creative fashion that is is satisfying to the user.


  • Create an aesthetically pleasing interface that does not take away from the functionality: Is the application aesthetically pleasing without being overwhelming or gimmicky?

    We would like to ensure that the application helps its users without being overwhelming. Ideally, the application will be helpful, enjoyable, and simple.
